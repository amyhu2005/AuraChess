Welcome to Aura Chess

What is Aura Chess?

Aura Chess is an advanced strategic move planner designed to visualize board control and overlapping piece dominance rather than just standard valid moves.

Visualization Modes

  • Winning Square Overlay: Shades a physical tile Blue if White pieces exert heavier absolute firepower over it, and Red if Black is dominating it.
  • Comprehensive X-Ray: Standard chess vision stops at the first piece. X-Ray mode allows sliding pieces (Queens, Rooks, Bishops) to pierce straight through solid matter, revealing true battery pressure behind both enemy and friendly lines.
  • Filter Trivial Dominance: Suppresses coverage dot tracking for any square covered by only ONE piece total, visually wiping away "obvious" noise to help you isolate dynamically contested hotspots across the board.

Granular Filters

Source Pieces: Use the piece list to selectively disable specific active pieces to remove their vectors strictly from coverage calculations.

Target Squares: Use the interactive 8x8 minimap to isolate specific focus zones—only squares highlighted "ON" in the minimap will display their dense attacker loadouts!
